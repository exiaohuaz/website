Where I'm from

Born in Pittsburgh, raised in Laramie, WY, studied a couple years in Beijing, then finishing highschool in Pittsburgh, the question of where I'm from is a rather convoluted one. Not to mention my official residence is in California, where I've visited but never lived.

Despite this, I love the dense greenery in Pittsburgh, close-knit communities and vast skies in Wyoming, the amazing food of China, and the diversity and modernity of the West Coast. I've been blessed with experiencing this multitude of cultures, and I consider all of them my home.

Hobbies

Especially during high school, I invested myself into art and piano. While I never considered them to be my career, I resolved to maintain a skill level comparable to those who actually study art and music. 

I enjoy finding niche skills such as pen spinning, puzzle solving, slack lining (basically a tight rope), juggling, origami, and more.

Currently, I spend my free time cooking, travelling or exploring the city, bouldering, hiking, or playing badminton.
